Project Analysis

Truffles and Trifles Redesign

Site Intent:

The site Truffles and Trifles at first glance gets the intended point across as a informational site for cooking classes and fresh baked pastries in the Orlando area. It will be the intent of this redesign to give the site a new fresh more streamline look as well as approach to mobile first design. Applying a responsive aspect to the site will give site users who are using any type of search medium, from smart phones to tablets and so on, the right look and usability that their operating system complies with. That way the site will reach across the board to help user flow and keep interest in what Truffles and Trifles has to offer.

Competitive Analysis:

When searching the competing market for cooking classes on sites such as Yelp.com and Alexa.com it becomes clear that Truffles and Trifles offers a less academic type of class. Which is in a realm of its own market wise, offering more casual lessons not to be confused with any less of a learning experience but more geared toward person’s who are looking to improve the cooking at home. All the other sites found are geared to a more academic professional career in cooking. This can be used to our advantage when redesigning Truffles and Trifles as they offer a unique approach to this type of learning demographic.

Competitor Analysis shows the sites keyword focus should stick to cooking lessons rather than cooking classes as to lead the user to understand the casual nature of the classes. Another thing that will help keep user interest and speaks for itself is creating links to the reviews written on sites such as Yelp and Angie’s List. This will help with the only thing I found as a bad review on Truffles and Trifles being confused as more school oriented and really just for a demographic that wants to be a better cook at home and is interested in gourmet foods and gifts. This will boost sales in not only cooking lessons but also the other goods that are offered by Truffles and Trifles giving a great return on this sites remodel investment.

Targeted Audience:

The Targeted Audience will stick to two types as to narrow the sites over wording of whom they are and what they have to offer. The main target will be the casual student who wants to improve his/her culinary skills and the second target will be a consumer of baked goods and gourmet food gifts. This will reduce the sites bloated wording and overall site system giving a fresh new approach that other competitors in this market have obtained.
